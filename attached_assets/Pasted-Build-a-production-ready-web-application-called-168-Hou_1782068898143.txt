Build a production-ready web application called **168 Hours**.

## Core Philosophy

Every person has exactly 168 hours per week.

This application is designed for personal use to understand where every hour of the week goes.

The goal is not task management.

The goal is weekly life analysis, visualization, awareness, and improvement.

Users should instantly see:

* Where their time goes
* How much they sleep
* How much they work
* How much they learn
* How much time is wasted
* Weekly patterns
* Long-term trends

The design should feel like a premium productivity product.
